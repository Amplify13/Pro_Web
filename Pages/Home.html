Lostwave Music

What is Lost Media? What is Lost Wave?

Lost Media is defined today as any media unavailable on the internet or to the general public. This includes songs, documents, books, cartoons, advertisements, video games, videos, etc. Lost Wave is a genre of music encapsulating specifically lost songs.
